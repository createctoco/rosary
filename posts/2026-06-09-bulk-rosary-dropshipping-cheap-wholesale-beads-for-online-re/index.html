Why wooden rosaries for dropshipping?
#

Plastic rosaries are everywhere. They’re shiny, they break, and they feel wrong in the hand. Wood is different. Our beads are real solid wood — not that plastic imitation stuff with a fake wood grain printed on. Each bead is hand-carved and polished. You can feel the difference.

And honestly? Wood sells better online. It photographs well, it smells good (some of our rosewood beads have a light natural fragrance), and it’s light for shipping.

The sizes that actually move
#

We make rosaries in 6mm, 8mm, 10mm, and 12mm beads.

The 8mm size? That’s our best seller. 10mm comes a close second. 6mm is small — some people like it for travel or for kids. 12mm is chunky, more for display or older hands. If you’re just starting out, stock 8mm and 10mm. You’ll sell more.

Knots matter more than you think
#

We can do Peruvian knot or flat knot — depends on what your customers expect. Peruvian knot is tighter, more traditional. Flat knot lies flatter against the hand. Some resellers ask for one over the other. We can switch it up.

If you’re dropshipping, you probably want the standard knot. But if you’re doing custom orders, tell us what you need.

Dropshipping specific tips
#

Here’s what I’ve learned from talking to resellers:

  • Shipping cost kills small orders. Our rosaries are light — about 30-50 grams each — so shipping 12 pieces is cheap. But if you’re shipping one at a time, it adds up. Some resellers buy 120 pieces and ship from their own location.
  • Product photos matter. We provide real photos, not stock images. You can use them. If you need a specific angle, ask.
  • Returns are rare. Wood rosaries don’t break easily. And if one does, we replace it. We’ve been doing this long enough to know that quality control matters.
