### One More Thing About Color Assortments

Don't just pick random colors. Think about your audience.

Older parishioners: brown, black, dark blue. They don't want flashy.
Young adults: bright colors, mixed beads, something that stands out.
Kids: whatever's cheapest, honestly. They lose them.
Men: dark wood, simple crucifix, no frills.
Women: rose, pink, light blue, cream.

We offer assorted packs where you get 10 colors in one order. That's usually the best approach. Let people pick what they like.
