### Why Go Double-Sided?

Let’s be honest. Most Catholic pendant suppliers cut corners. They’ll do a single-sided design, then fill the back with a flat cross or a vague texture. That’s fine if you’re selling to a souvenir shop. But for churches, retreat centers, or wholesale buyers who want something that actually gets worn daily? You need both sides to mean something.

Our double-sided Sacred Heart pendant measures 25mm by 18mm. It’s not too big for a rosary centerpiece, but it’s substantial enough to stand out on a chain. The brass is 3mm thick. We don’t use that thin sheet metal you see in gift shop bins. That stuff bends if you look at it wrong.

We had a client from a Catholic bookstore chain in the Philippines who originally ordered a single-sided version from another supplier. After six months, they said the backs were tarnishing unevenly. So they switched to us. We sent them a revised quote, and they took 500 pieces of the double-sided Sacred Heart. Now they reorder every quarter. No complaints on tarnish — because we lacquer coat each pendant by hand, not by spray gun.
