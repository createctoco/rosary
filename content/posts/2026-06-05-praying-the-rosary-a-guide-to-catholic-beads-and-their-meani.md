### Crucifix and Centerpiece Standards

Every Catholic rosary must include a crucifix and a centerpiece (typically a medallion of Mary or a saint). ### Bead Count and Layout Accuracy

A traditional Catholic rosary consists of five decades (sets of ten Hail Mary beads) separated by one Our Father bead, plus an introductory cross and a few additional beads. The total bead count should be precisely 59 beads (including the crucifix and centerpiece).
