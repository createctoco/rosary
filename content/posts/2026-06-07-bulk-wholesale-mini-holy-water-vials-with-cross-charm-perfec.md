## Size and Style Options for Vials

Not all holy water vials are the same. We do a few different styles.

- **The classic tear-drop shape.** Most popular. Easy to hold, easy to fill.
- **The round vial.** Smaller, more discreet. Fits in a pocket.
- **The flat vial.** Good for stacking in packaging. Saves on shipping space.

The vials come with a cork or a screw cap. Cork looks more traditional. Screw cap is more practical — no accidental leaks in the mail.

## A Few Practical Tips from Our Shipping Experience

We’ve shipped a lot of these. Here’s what we’ve learned.

- **Glass vials break.** We pack them tight with foam inserts. But if you’re shipping internationally, consider plastic vials instead. They’re less breakable, and they still look fine.
- **Corks can dry out.** If you’re storing them for months, keep them in a cool place. Or go with the screw cap.
- **Holy water evaporates.** Tell your customers to seal the vial tight and keep it out of direct sunlight. We learned this the hard way.
