## Knots Matter More Than You Think

Here's something most buyers don't consider: the knot.

We can do Peruvian knot or flat knot between beads. Peruvian knot is tighter, more uniform. Flat knot is traditional — what you see in most church rosaries. Both work. But for Christmas decorative rosaries — the kind people hang on trees or drape over nativity scenes — we usually recommend flat knot. It lays flatter against surfaces.

You can also customize the cord color. Red and gold for Christmas. Green and white for Advent. We've done a run of purple and silver for Lent last year, but that's a different story.

The point is: we're not a one-size-fits-all factory. Tell us what you need.
