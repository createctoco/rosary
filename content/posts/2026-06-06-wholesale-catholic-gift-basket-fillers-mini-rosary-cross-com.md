Let’s be real.  Not everyone wants a giant rosary hanging from their rearview mirror.  A lot of folks prefer something discreet they can slip into a pocket or a purse.  That’s the beauty of the mini combo.  It’s small enough to be unobtrusive, but it still serves its purpose as a prayer aid.

We’ve also seen a spike in demand from Catholic schools.  They use these for First Communion favors, or for the kids to hand out during May Crowning.
