## Selecting the Right Crucifix for Wholesale Rosary Assembly

The crucifix is the most significant component of any rosary, as it focuses the faithful on the sacrifice of Christ. For wholesale buyers, the crucifix must meet both liturgical and commercial standards. A proper Catholic crucifix features the corpus (body of Christ) with a titulus (INRI sign) and a design that is reverent, not merely decorative. When sourcing in bulk, consider material options: die-stamped brass crucifixes offer affordability for large church orders, while zinc alloy or pewter crucifixes provide a heavier, more premium feel for gift shops. The finish is equally important—matte silver, bright gold, or antique oxidized finishes each appeal to different demographics. Our inventory includes crucifixes ranging from 2 inches to 4 inches in length, suitable for wrist rosaries, standard chaplets, or decorative wall crucifixes.
